Our Story
There was a time that a girl drove 300 miles back home every weekend to help her mom use technology to apply for Social Security for Disability. She then started to wonder whether a local college student could have teach her mom to use the computer instead.
With the support of the Engineering Innovation Institute at the University of Florida, the girl started a tech support company for seniors that focuses on customer growth, named Orchard.
