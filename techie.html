Join The Team
As a techie, not only will you be joining the Orchard family, you will also be entering the lives of every customer you come across. Often, you will be their safety net and shining light while they discover how technology could augment their abilities and enhance their lives. If we have to get more cheesy about this, we think of Orchard as the powerhouse that charges and fuels our techies, so you could go out and bring happiness to the lives that you touched.
- Ming Yang, Founder
Why Should You?
Flexible Schedule
Work when you can, however much you want. You will travel to homes and help customers during the hours you specified.
Technical Experience
We will help you master the troubleshooting mindset, something that will take you far in any career.
High Impact
Our work literally change lives. No matter what the work is, you will always directly make someone's life better.
Want More Reasons?
How About These?
Communication Skills
We don't just communicate, we obsess over communicating effectively and efficiently, such as explaining complex techincal concepts using simple terms.
Boost Resume
Combining your technical and soft skills to change peoples' lives? We think you will have plenty of stories to ace those future interviews with.
Gain Persepctive
Sometimes we like to step back from our day-to-day rush, and absorb all that life has to teach us, such as experiencing bits of someone else's life.
